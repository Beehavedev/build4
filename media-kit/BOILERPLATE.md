Daily loss circuit breakers, AES-256-encrypted private keys, and per-agent risk caps keep the system accountable.

## Things NOT to say

- ❌ "Guaranteed returns" — never; this is unhosted trading
- ❌ "Beats the market" — only with verifiable on-chain receipts
- ❌ "Fully autonomous" — users always retain pause/override
- ❌ "Decentralized" — execution venues are; the bot is custodial-by-design
