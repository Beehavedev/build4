GLOBAL RULES — apply to every tab:
Never show these to regular users:

"Swarm Agreement — admin token required"
"Swarm Cost & Speed — No swarm telemetry recorded"
Any raw error messages or stack traces
Admin-only data behind auth walls

Always show:

Wallet address with one-tap copy on Wallet tab
Which system each balance belongs to (Aster vs BSC vs 42.space — never just "$0.00" with no context)
One clear next action per screen when the user has nothing yet ("Fund Aster", "Browse Markets", "Create Agent")

Navigation bar labels:

Home → Home
Agents → Agents
Wallet → Wallet
Portfolio → Portfolio
Predictions → Predict

Tab bar active state: purple underline, icon fills purple. Inactive: gray icon only, no label color.

The test: show this app to someone who has never heard of BUILD4. Within 10 seconds they should know: (1) how much money they have, (2) what the two trading systems are, (3) what to do next. If they can't answer those three things in 10 seconds, the design has failed.
Deliver all 5 tab components updated, type-check clean, mobile-first, no console errors.
